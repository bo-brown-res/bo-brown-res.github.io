About Me

I'm a current Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Professor Mark Craven.

Before joining UW-Madison, I worked as a software engineer. I received my M.S. in Computer Science at the University of Southern California.

Research Interests

My research interests center on applications of machine learning to healthcare, with an emphasis on interpretable machine learning and time series analysis.
